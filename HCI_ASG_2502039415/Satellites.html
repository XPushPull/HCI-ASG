Satellites

When you hear the word satellite, you will usually imagine a machine flying in space. However, the meaning of satellites is not only that.Reporting from the NASA page, satellites are objects that orbit or surround other objects in space. There are two different types of satellites: natural and artificial. According to Dr. Allan McInnes, a satellite is anything that orbits around a larger object. Natural satellites are celestial bodies in space that orbit around larger bodies. The moon is called a natural satellite because it orbits the planet. Meanwhile, artificial satellites are satellites made by humans and launched into orbit using rockets. There are thousands of artificial satellites orbiting the Earth.
